Selling Cannabis Step-by-Step

1 Register a Business Click to expand
  • You must have a registered business or another legal entity to apply for a cannabis retail licence.
  • The province will review and approve the name of your business. It can not contain words like pharmacy, apothecary, or dispensary to suggest your business sells medical cannabis.

Read more about  registering a business for cannabis

 2 Create a Business BCeID Account Click to expand
  • A Business BCeID account is required to login to the application.
  • It takes about 15 minutes to complete the 5 step registration process. To begin, visit Register for a Business BCeID

    • Read more about  Business BCeID

 3 Contact Your Local Government or First Nation Click to expand
  • It's up to local governments and First Nations to decide if they'll allow cannabis retail stores.
  • Be sure to check with your local authority before applying. The province will not issue a cannabis retail licence in a community that doesn't support it.
  • Local government or First Nation support does not mean your business has a local business licence or that it has been zoned for cannabis sales. Check with your local authority about what to do next.

    • Read more about local government and First Nation involvement
