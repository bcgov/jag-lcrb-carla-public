Property Ownership or Lease

At minimum, you must have an offer to purchase, lease or sub-lease the property when you submit your application.

Before you receive approval in principle, you will be asked to submit a fully executed lease, sub-lease or certificate of title. The Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch will contact you if further information is required.
