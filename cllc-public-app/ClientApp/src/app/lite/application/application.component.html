Applicants must pay a $7500 fee to apply for a cannabis retail store licence. The fee includes the cost of the initial required security screening and financial integrity checks. It is not refundable.

 

Proceed to pay online using your credit card through the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch (LCRB) secure web portal.
