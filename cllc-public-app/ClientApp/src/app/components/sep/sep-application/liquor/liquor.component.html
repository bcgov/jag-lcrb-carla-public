Please provide the amount and type of drinks that will be served and/or sold at your event. This information helps you estimate appropriate serving quantities for your event and, if you are selling drinks, provides the most accurate calculation of Provincial Sales Tax (PST). The quantities you provide are subject to review and approval and may be used by police and liquor inspectors during inspections.

Many police jurisdictions limit drink quantities for a special event based on the hours of liquor service and the number of patrons at the event. Please contact your local police detachment for this information, before you enter the drink quantity on the application. Remember, you must take precautions to prevent over-service and intoxication of patrons.
