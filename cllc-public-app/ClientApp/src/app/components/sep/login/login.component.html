Option 1: BCeID

There are two types of BCeID:

  • A Basic BCeID account is used to log in to services online. You must use a username and password. You just need to have an email address.
  • A Business BCeID account is used to log in to services online when representing a business or organization. You must have:
    • Documentation to prove the business or organization's identity
    • An email address

Learn more about BCeID.
Option 2: BC Services Card

A BC Services Card Login is used to log in to services online. You must either:

  • Have a BC Services Card
or
  • Live in Canada and have ID issued within Canada

You can use either:

  • The BC Services Card app on your mobile device
or
  • A username, password and BC Token

Learn more about BC Services Card.
