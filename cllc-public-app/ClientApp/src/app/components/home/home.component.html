Apply for a liquor or cannabis licence

You must have a Business BCeID to log into the online licensing portal.

Through the online licensing portal you can:

  • Apply for new liquor and non-medical cannabis licences;
  • Renew existing licences and apply for changes or endorsements to your licence;
  • Pay application, renewal and other fees online;
  • Manage your account and contact information; and
  • Submit permanent licensee changes and personal history summary forms;

You can find a complete list of the types of liquor licences that are available through the portal on the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch’s main website.

Due to the increase in demand related to the COVID-19 pandemic, please expect delays in the standard processing time for all licensing applications.

We are aware of a login issue impacting certain versions of Google Chrome.

If you encounter a warning message that says "Your connection is not fully secure" try using a different browser like Mozilla Firefox, or update your version of Chrome.

The Liquor and Cannabis Licensing portal is closed for maintenance. During this time, you will not be able to log in to the site.

We apologize for any inconvenience.
