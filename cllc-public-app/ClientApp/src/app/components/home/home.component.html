Apply for a liquor or cannabis licence

You must have a Business BCeID to log into the online licensing portal.

Through the Liquor and cannabis licensing portal you can:

  • Apply for new liquor and non-medical cannabis licences;
  • Renew existing licences and apply for changes or endorsements to your licence;
  • Pay application, renewal and other fees online;
  • Manage your account and contact information; and
  • Submit permanent licensee changes and personal history summary forms;

Food primary, Liquor primary, Agent licences

The LCRB is committed to providing excellent service and support as part of your transition to our digital licensing environment. As there are a large volume of licensees moving online, we ask that you be patient with the process and technology as we support you through this transition. If you have any questions about this change, encounter account-information issues, or need help accessing or using the new portal at any time, please email our Client Support Team at LCRBLiquor@gov.bc.ca, or call 1-866-209-2111 (toll-free in Canada) or 250-952-5787 (in Victoria).
