Apply for a liquor or cannabis licence

You must have a Business BCeID to log into the online licensing portal.

Through the Liquor and cannabis licensing portal you can:

  • Apply for new liquor and non-medical cannabis licences;
  • Renew existing licences and apply for changes or endorsements to your licence;
  • Pay application, renewal and other fees online;
  • Manage your account and contact information; and
  • Submit permanent licensee changes and personal history summary forms;

If you have any questions or need help using the liquor and cannabis licensing portal, please contact us.

The Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch (LCRB) will begin accepting Producer Retail Store licence applications on November 30, 2022. Please do not attempt to apply online before this date.
