Complete the following forms and email them, along with supporting documents, to Cannabis.Licensing@gov.bc.ca. Supporting documents are listed in the forms. Include your private corporation’s name in the subject line of the email.

  • Business Financial Integrity Form
  • Associate Organization Assessment Form: Corporation

  • Officers
