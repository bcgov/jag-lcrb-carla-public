Have the details of your {{ account.getBusinessTypeName() }} changed?

Licensees and authorization holders must notify the LCRB of any changes to their structure within 10 days. These changes include:

  • Internal or external share transfers (including amalgamations)
  • Name changes of associated businesses
  • Name changes of individuals associated with your {{ account.getBusinessTypeName() }}
  • Entering Receivership
  • Specifying an Executor
  • Change to Tied House
