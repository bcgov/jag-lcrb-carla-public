This dashboard allows applicants to submit applications for liquor and non-medical cannabis licences in British Columbia.

Please note that the Liquor and Cannabis Regulation Branch (LCRB) will only process complete applications. Applications which are submitted without all required information or documents will not be processed. Applicants will be contacted and provided the opportunity to submit missing information. Once applicants provide all required documents to the LCRB, the application will be processed.

RENEW A LIQUOR OR CANNABIS LICENCE

To renew your Liquor or Cannabis licence visit your Licences & Authorizations page in the licensing portal. Note: You should not submit a new licence application to complete a renewal.

Visit your Licences & Authorizations page on the licensing portal to submit changes to your licence, request authorizations or download a new copy of your licence for your records.

Visit your Account Profile page to update your contact preferences.

Apply to transfer cannabis products between licences

Cannabis retail store licensees must complete the Application for Cannabis Products Transfer Form and email it to LCRB.Product.Transfers@gov.bc.ca if they wish to transfer cannabis products between their retail stores

Written Approval from the LCRB must be recieved by a licencee before initiaing product transfer.

All cannabis retail store licence holders must follow the terms and conditions of their licence as set out in the Retail Store Licence Terms and Conditions Handbook .
